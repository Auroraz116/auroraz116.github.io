Songs that carry memories

Posted on 2019-06-17

Recently I’m pretty active in my music zone, because there are some amazing songs that catch me. At the beginning of this semester, I usually listened to some quick songs from Taylor, but it always makes me feel uneasy. Although I have to admit that Taylor is a great singer and for me, she is absolutely top one on the singer list in America.

However, we all know that only songs that suit you best is the greatest music during that specific period.

These days, I really got a crush on JJ Lin’s new song(故事细腻). To my surprise, there are French in this song that gives it a special characteristic. This song uses a minor key and creates a peaceful and beautiful sense of loss…maybe it’s not about losing something, it’s about process and present. However, I still smelled the melancholy and a touch of sadness.

Every time I listen to his songs, it always gives me sad feelings. I don’t know whether because it had already left a deep impression on me when I first got to know him or something else.
